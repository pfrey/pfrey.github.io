Four-Toed Hedgehog

Hedgehogs are lactose intolerant.

  • Scientific Name: Atelerix albiventris
  • Average Length: 15 and 25 centimetres
  • Average Lifespan: 4-6 years
  • Habitat: Grassy environments or open woodland

The four-toed hedgehog is one of the most popular species of domesticated hedgehogs. They are solitary and nocturnal animals. They generally stay on the ground foraging for insects, grubs, snails, spiders, and the like. But they are capable of swimming and climbing, if it becomes necessary.
